Instructions

  • You are Player 1 (Green Icon).
  • Randomly either Green or Red will get chance to play first.
  • Step 1: Moving one's piece to a neighboring (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) position that contains a square but not the opponent's piece
  • Step 2 : Removing any square with no piece on it
  • Step 3 : Click Confirm buttom to confirm your moves
  • The player who cannot make any move loses the game.
